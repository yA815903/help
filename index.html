How to Use

Welcome to Assistive Apps! This app provides three main features:

  • Voice-Controlled Assistant: Use voice commands to control your device.
  • AR Subtitles: Get real-time subtitles for conversations.
  • Image to Speech: Take a picture and have the text read to you.
